Research Methodology

The WARI methodology employs a comprehensive 10-factor assessment framework validated through extensive peer review and empirical testing. Our approach combines expert analysis with quantitative modeling to produce reliable automation risk scores.

10-Factor Assessment Framework

1

Task Complexity

Cognitive and physical complexity assessment

2

Human Interaction

Interpersonal communication requirements

3

Creative Problem Solving

Innovation and creative thinking needs

4

Pattern Recognition

Routine vs. adaptive pattern analysis

5

Physical Dexterity

Fine motor skills and coordination

6

Emotional Intelligence

Empathy and emotional reasoning

7

Adaptability

Response to changing circumstances

8

Decision Making

Complex judgment and decision complexity

9

Learning Requirements

Continuous learning and skill adaptation

10

Regulatory Constraints

Legal and regulatory automation barriers

API Documentation & Downloads

WARI API v1.0

Access WARI data programmatically through our RESTful API. Perfect for researchers, developers, and organizations building automation impact tools.

GET /api/occupations

Retrieve all occupation data with WARI scores and projections

curl -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" https://api.wakel.io/v1/occupations

GET /api/sectors

Get sector-level aggregated WARI statistics

curl -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" https://api.wakel.io/v1/sectors

GET /api/occupations/{id}

Retrieve detailed information for a specific occupation

curl -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" https://api.wakel.io/v1/occupations/data-scientist
